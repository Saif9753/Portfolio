About

I am a visual artist with a deep passion for both traditional miniature painting and contemporary animation. My artistic journey blends the meticulous, historical craft of miniature art with the dynamic, narrative-driven world of animation. Drawing from the rich traditions of South Asian miniatures, I create detailed, vibrant artworks that tell stories of culture, history, and imagination. At the same time, I embrace animation as a medium to infuse my art with movement, emotion, and modern storytelling techniques.
